Badhiya! Website banana ek accha kadam hai. Par website alag-alag tarah ki hoti hain aur unke banne ka tarika bhi alag hota hai. Aapko kaisi website chahiye, yeh batane ke liye mujhe thodi aur jaankari chahiye hogi. Kuch sawal jinse hum shuruat kar sakte hain: 1. **Udeshya (Purpose):** Aap website kisliye banana chahte hain? * Apni personal jaankari/portfolio share karne ke liye? (Personal blog/portfolio?) * Apne business ke products ya services bechne ke liye? (For business to sell products/services?) * Koi information share karne ke liye, jaise blog ya news site? (To share information, like a blog or news site?) * Online store ya e-commerce jahan log saman kharid sakein? (Online store or e-commerce where people can buy things?) * Ya kuch aur? (Or something else?) 2. **Features (Kya-kya hoga usmein):** Aapki website par kya-kya hona chahiye? * Sirf kuch pages jismein jaankari ho? (Just a few pages with information?) * Contact form? (Contact form?) * Gallery ya photo album? (Gallery or photo album?) * Product list aur shopping cart? (Product list and shopping cart?) * Booking system? (Booking system?) * Blog section jahan aap articles likh sakein? (Blog section where you can write articles?) * Login/registration ki suvidha? (Login/registration facility?) 3. **Budget (Kharche ka anumaan):** Aap is par kitna kharch karna chahte hain ya kar sakte hain? * Kam se kam kharche mein? (At minimum cost?) * Thoda zyada invest kar sakte hain? (Can invest a bit more?) 4. **Khud banana hai ya banwana hai? (DIY or Professional?):** Kya aap khud website banana chahte hain (agar thodi technical jaankari ho) ya kisi professional se banwana chahte hain? 5. **Timeline:** Aapko website kab tak chahiye? --- **Jab aap in sawalon ka jawab denge, toh main aapko behtar tareeke se guide kar paunga ki aapke liye sabse accha option kya rahega:** * **Agar aapko simple aur kam kharche mein chahiye, aur aap khud thoda effort kar sakte hain:** * **Website Builders:** Jaise Wix, Squarespace, Google Sites, ya Shopify (agar e-commerce hai). Yeh drag-and-drop hote hain aur technical knowledge ki zyada zarurat nahi hoti. Inmein monthly subscription hota hai. * **Agar aapko zyada control, flexibility aur apne hisaab se design chahiye:** * **WordPress (Self-hosted):** Yeh sabse popular platform hai. Ismein aapko hosting aur domain kharidna padta hai. Thodi technical jaankari chahiye hoti hai ya aap kisi se banwa sakte hain. * **Agar aapko ekdum professional, custom website chahiye aur aapke paas accha budget hai:** * **Web Developer/Agency hire karein:** Yeh sabse mahanga option hota hai, par aapko exactly waisi website milti hai jaisi aap chahte hain. Toh bataiye, aapke mann mein kya hai?
